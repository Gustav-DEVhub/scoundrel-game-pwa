    How to Play Scoundrel

    1

    The goal

    You are a scoundrel fighting through a dungeon one room at a time. Clear every card in the deck without losing all your 20 HP to win.

    2

    Each room

    Four cards are dealt. Select 3 of them and press Face Selected to resolve them. The card you leave behind carries into the next room — choose wisely.

    3

    Card types

    • ♥ Potion — restores HP equal to its value (up to 20 HP max). Only the first potion heals each room; any extras are wasted.
    • ♦ Weapon — equip it to reduce monster damage. Picking up a new weapon replaces your old one.
    • ♣ ♠ Monster — deals damage equal to its value. A weapon reduces that damage (see below).
    4

    Fighting with a weapon

    When you fight a monster, damage = monster value − weapon value (minimum 0).

    ⚠️ Your weapon can only be used against a monster whose value is strictly lower than the last one it defeated. If the monster is equal or stronger, your weapon breaks and you take full damage.

    Example: ♦7 defeats ♣9 → 2 damage taken, last defeated = 9. Next fight: ♣8 ✔ (weapon works) · ♣9 ✘ (weapon breaks, 9 damage).

    5

    Avoiding a room

    Press Avoid Room to send all 4 cards to the bottom of the deck and draw a new room. You cannot avoid two rooms in a row.

    6

    Victory & defeat

    • 🏆 Win — empty the deck. Score = HP + equipped weapon value.
    • 💀 Lose — reach 0 HP. Score = negative total value of monsters still in the deck.

    The deck

    44 cards — a standard deck with red face cards and red Aces removed:

    • ♣ ♠ 2–A → 26 monsters
    • ♦ 2–10 → 9 weapons
    • ♥ 2–10 → 9 potions
